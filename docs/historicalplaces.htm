Some local places in Eskişehir

Old Houses in Odunpazarı

Odunpazarı (literally "firewood market" in Turkish), is a historic district of Eskişehir Province in the Central Anatolia region of Turkey. Odunpazarı is one of the central districts of Eskişehir along with the district of Tepebaşı. You can see the historical houses which is on the UNESCO world heritage list located in Odunpazarı below.

Museum of Modern Glass Art

Museum of Modern Glass Art is a museum dedicated to contemporary glass art. The museum was established with the cooperation of Eskişehir Metropolitan Municipality, Anadolu University and a private group for friends of glass in 2007. Turkey's first museum of its kind, it exhibits works of 58 domestic and 10 foreign artists.


Haller Youth Center

Haller Youth Center is a culture and art center built with the restoration of the historical "Fruit and Vegetable Hall" located in Eskişehir. While Fruit and Vegetable Hall was planning to be demolished because it was not used, in the year of 2000, it was restored to be a shopping and entertainment center with the support of Prof. Dr. Yılmaz Büyükerşen. The building is dominated by wood, wrought iron and natural stone. It was inspired by 'Covent Garden' in London (UK) and flower-shaped building in Hamburg (Germany).
